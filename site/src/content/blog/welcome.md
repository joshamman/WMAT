People often tell me, in the first session, that they "can't even draw a stick figure." That's okay. Truly. Art therapy was never about the drawing.

It's about the **process** of making — the way color, texture, and motion can hold what words can't quite reach yet. You don't need talent. You only need a willingness to show up and make a mark.

## What a first session looks like

We talk a little about what brought you in. We get comfortable with the materials. And then we begin — gently, at your pace. There's no pressure to create anything in particular, and nothing you make is judged.

> Begin where you are. That's always enough.
